SISTERHOOD



Big/Little

Big Little is when the new pledge class is connected with a member that is a year older then them to act as a “big sister” in the soroity. This connection is so helpful in meeting new people and making long lasting relationships.


Time Together

Throughout the year our sisterhood chair schedules events for our members to spend some time together. These events in the past have been arts and Crafts Night, Movie Night, and also events like Parents Weekend.



Trips

We love to go to local places as a chapter to spend time together and also to support local buisnesses. Events like this can include a trip to the pumpkin patch at Halloween or going to get ice cream at Mapel View
